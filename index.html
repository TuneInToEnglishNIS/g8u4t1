AFTER LISTENING

Task 2 :Imagine you are the singer of the song “Don't you know how busy and important I am?” Write a journal entry expressing your thoughts and feelings about being constantly busy and its impact on your life. Consider the following questions in your reflection:

- How do you feel about always being busy and having so much to do?
- Do you think being busy makes you feel important or overwhelmed?
- Have you ever turned down opportunities or experiences because you were too busy? How did it make you feel?
